About NYClimber

Rock climbing is an activity in which participants climb up, down or across natural rock formations or artificial rock walls. Here at NYClimber we want to introduce this to High School student so they can spend their time learning skills and having fun. Rock climbing is a physically and mentally demanding sport, one that often tests a climber's strength, endurance, agility and balance along with mental control. We offer a feeling that will redefine what you've always thought about Rock Climbing and make sure its an activity that you'll want to do for a long time.

Safety at NYClimber

Safety is our #1 priority at NYClimber, we do our best to make sure that students learn the safe ways to climb along with having proper gear. We provide our students with plenty of safety adivce like...

  • Always Check Harnesses
  • Always Wear A Climbing Helmet
  • Always Pay Attention
  • Always Climb With The Rope Over Your Leg
  • Always Use Safe Anchors
